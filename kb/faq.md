# Frequently Asked Questions

## Pricing & Plans

### What's included in each plan?
See `config/pricing.json` for complete details. Basic includes core features, Core adds priority support and advanced features, Premium includes everything plus custom integrations and dedicated account manager.

### Can I get a discount?
We offer standard pricing only. No discounts or custom pricing available.

### What's the onboarding fee?
Standard onboarding is included at no additional cost. Priority onboarding is available for Premium plans.

## Features & Customization

### Can you customize the platform for our needs?
No. We offer standardized, proven workflows only. Custom development is not available.

### Can you add a feature we need?
We don't take custom feature requests. Our product team prioritizes based on strategic goals and broad customer feedback.

### Do you have a roadmap?
We don't share roadmaps or timelines for future features. Our focus is on delivering value with current capabilities.

## Refunds

### What's your refund policy?
Contact support for refund inquiries. Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts.

## Support

### What support is included?
- Basic: Email support, business hours
- Core: Priority email support, extended hours
- Premium: 24/7 support, dedicated account manager

### How do I get help?
Email support@doublevision.company or use the in-app support feature.

## Technical

### Do you offer API access?
Yes, API access is available on Core and Premium plans.

### Can we integrate with our systems?
Standard integrations are available on Core and Premium plans. Custom integrations are not supported.

### Is there an on-premise option?
No, we're cloud-only. All deployments are SaaS.
