# Double Vision Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette (Grayscale)

**Primary Colors**:
- Black: `#000000`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

**Grayscale Scale**:
- Gray 50: `#FAFAFA` (lightest)
- Gray 100: `#F5F5F5`
- Gray 200: `#E5E5E5`
- Gray 300: `#D4D4D4`
- Gray 400: `#A3A3A3`
- Gray 500: `#737373`
- Gray 600: `#525252`
- Gray 700: `#404040`
- Gray 800: `#262626`
- Gray 900: `#171717` (darkest)

## Typography

**Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- Style: Clean, modern, professional

## Logo Usage

**Primary Logo**: `assets/double-vision-logo.PNG`
- Use on light backgrounds (white/gray)
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Minimum size: 100px width

**Product Logos**:
- ShopWare: `assets/shopware-logo.svg`
- Dealio: `assets/dealio-logo.svg`
- Quotely: `assets/quotely-logo.svg` (if exists)

## Design Principles

1. **Minimalism**: Clean, uncluttered layouts
2. **Contrast**: High contrast (black on white, white on black)
3. **Consistency**: Uniform spacing, typography, colors
4. **Professional**: Business-focused, trustworthy aesthetic

## Graphic Templates

### Social Media Post
- Size: 1200x630px
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Black (#000000) or Gray (#525252)
- Font: Inter Bold 64px (title), Inter Regular 32px (subtitle)

### Email Header
- Size: 600x200px
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Black (#000000)
- Font: Inter Bold 36px (title), Inter Regular 18px (subtitle)

### Blog Featured Image
- Size: 1200x675px
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Black (#000000)
- Font: Inter Bold 56px (title), Inter Regular 20px (meta)

### Ad Creative
- Size: 1200x628px
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Black (#000000)
- Font: Inter Bold 48px (headline), Inter Regular 24px (body), Inter SemiBold 28px (CTA)

## Usage Rules

1. **Never use color** (except grayscale)
2. **Always use Inter font** (or system fallback)
3. **Maintain brand consistency** across all graphics
4. **Keep it simple** - less is more
5. **High contrast** for readability

## Examples

### Good ✅
- Black text on white background
- White text on black background
- High contrast grayscale combinations
- Clean, minimal layouts

### Bad ❌
- Colorful graphics
- Multiple fonts
- Low contrast combinations
- Cluttered designs
