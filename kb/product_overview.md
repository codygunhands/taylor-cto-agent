# Product Overview

## What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive platform solution designed for manufacturing and supply chain operations.

### Core Capabilities

- **ShopFlow**: Production workflow management
- **ShopLink**: RFQ and supplier network
- **ShopCalc Pro**: Advanced manufacturing calculations

### Supported Use Cases

- Manufacturing job tracking
- Supplier relationship management
- RFQ posting and bid management
- Production calculations and estimates

### Not Supported

- Custom software development
- Custom integrations beyond our standard API
- Industry-specific customizations
- White-label solutions
- On-premise deployments (cloud-only)

## Standard Offerings

All features are delivered as standardized, proven workflows. We do not offer custom development or modifications to our core platform.

## Integration Options

- Standard REST API (available on Core and Premium plans)
- Webhook support (Core and Premium)
- Standard data exports (all plans)

## Support Levels

- **Basic**: Email support, business hours
- **Core**: Priority email support, extended hours
- **Premium**: 24/7 support, dedicated account manager
